This specialized committee on the U.S. Supreme Court simulates the process of one of the most influential judicial bodies in the world. Delegates will assume the roles of U.S. Supreme Court justices, navigating partisan dynamics, debating legal issues, and coming to decisions on pressing domestic issues. This committee emphasizes collaboration, negotiation, and a deep understanding of American political structures to reflect the complexities of real-world governance.
